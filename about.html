About ToolHub

ToolHub is a collection of fast, privacy-friendly tools that solve everyday problems in seconds. We focus on lightweight tools that work directly in your browser and don't require signups.

Our mission

The internet is full of tools that are slow, filled with popups, or require accounts and subscriptions. ToolHub exists to provide a cleaner alternative: simple utilities that are easy to use and work well on mobile.

Whether you're compressing an image to match strict upload limits (20KB/50KB/100KB), creating an invoice for a client, or calculating salary conversions — ToolHub is built to be quick and practical.

What makes ToolHub different

  • Privacy-first: many tools run directly on your device (no file uploads needed).
  • Mobile-first: built for phones and quick tasks.
  • No signup: tools are accessible instantly.
  • Free forever: we keep access free so anyone can use it.

Tools available

Current tools on ToolHub include:

  • Image Compressor: compress images to target sizes like 20KB / 50KB / 100KB.
  • Invoice Generator: build a clean invoice and download a PDF.
  • Salary Calculator: convert hourly ↔ monthly ↔ annual salary quickly.
