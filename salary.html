FAQ

How is monthly salary calculated from hourly pay?

Monthly salary is estimated using your hourly rate, hours per week, and weeks per year. You can adjust work hours to match your schedule.

What is the difference between gross salary and net salary?

Gross salary is your pay before deductions (tax, PF, insurance). Net salary is what you actually receive after deductions.

Can I calculate part-time salary?

Yes. Enter your part-time hours per week (or hours per day) and the calculator will estimate monthly and annual salary.

Is this calculator accurate?

The result is an estimate based on the numbers you enter. Real salary can vary based on paid leave, unpaid leave, overtime, and tax deductions.
